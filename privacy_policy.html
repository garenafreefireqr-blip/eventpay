📋 Quick Summary This Privacy Policy explains how Event Pay Management collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application. By using our app, you agree to the terms described below.
1
📂

Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide you with our event payment management services. This includes:

Data Type Details Purpose
Personal Info Name, email address, phone number Account creation & communication
Event Data Event names, dates, participant lists Organizing and managing events
Payment Data Transaction amounts, payment status, contributor records Processing contributions & tracking
Device Info Device type, OS version, app version App performance & bug fixing
Usage Data App features used, session duration Improving user experience
2
⚙️

How We Use Your Data

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your account on the Event Pay Management platform
  • To process event contributions and payment tracking between participants
  • To send you notifications about event payment status and updates
  • To generate payment reports and summaries for event organizers
  • To provide customer support and resolve technical issues
  • To improve the app's features, performance, and user experience
  • To comply with legal obligations and prevent fraudulent activity
  • To send important service announcements and updates
✅ We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties, use your data for unrelated advertising, or share your payment information without your explicit consent.
3
💳

Payment Information

Event Pay Management handles financial contributions between event participants. Here is how we handle payment data:

  • We record contribution amounts, payer names, and payment status for event accounting purposes
  • Full payment card numbers are never stored on our servers — any card processing is handled by certified third-party payment gateways
  • Payment history is retained to provide accurate records for event organizers and contributors
  • Transaction data may be used to resolve disputes or generate financial reports for events
⚠️ Important: If you use third-party payment services (UPI, bank transfers, etc.) through our app, those services have their own privacy policies which we encourage you to review.
4
🤝

Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data only in these circumstances:

  • Within your Event Group: Event organizers can view the contribution status and names of participants in their managed events
  • Service Providers: We may share data with trusted technical partners (cloud hosting, analytics) who are contractually bound to protect your data
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government authority
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition, your data may be transferred as part of the business assets
  • With Your Consent: Any other sharing will only occur with your explicit permission
5
🔒

Data Security

We take the security of your personal and financial data seriously and implement industry-standard measures:

  • All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS protocols
  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plaintext
  • Access to user data is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments are conducted
  • We use secure cloud infrastructure with data backups and disaster recovery protocols
🛡️ Security Notice While we implement strong security practices, no method of data transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong passwords and keep your login credentials confidential.

✋

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: You can update or correct inaccurate information in your account settings
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your account and associated personal data
  • Data Portability: You can request your data in a portable format (CSV/JSON)
  • Opt-Out: You can opt out of non-essential communications at any time
  • Withdrawal of Consent: You may withdraw consent for data processing, though this may affect app functionality

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email address provided below. We will respond within 30 days.

7
🍪

Cookies & Tracking

Our app and associated web services may use the following tracking technologies:

  • Session Cookies: Used to keep you logged in during your session
  • Local Storage: Used to save your preferences and settings on your device
  • Analytics: We may use anonymized analytics to understand app usage patterns (no personally identifiable data is shared)

We do not use advertising cookies or cross-site tracking. You can clear cookies and local storage through your browser or device settings at any time.

8
👶

Children's Privacy

Event Pay Management is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take immediate steps to delete such information from our servers. If you believe a child has provided us with their data, please contact us immediately.

⚠️ This app is intended for use by adults and responsible individuals managing event finances. Users must be at least 13 years old to create an account.
9
🗓️

Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law:

  • Account data is retained while your account is active
  • Event & payment records are kept for up to 3 years for accounting and dispute resolution purposes
  • Deleted accounts: Data is permanently removed within 90 days of account deletion request
  • Backup copies may persist for up to 30 additional days in encrypted backup systems

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make significant changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • Notify active users through an in-app notification or email
  • Post a prominent notice in the app for at least 7 days

Your continued use of the Event Pay Management app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
